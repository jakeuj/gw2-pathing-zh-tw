## Recorded scope

The project treats the complete exchange as permission to create and publicly
redistribute a free, non-commercial `zh-TW` version of the current Tekkit's
All-In-One Marker Pack, and treats the final confirmation as explicit
permission to use, modify, and redistribute the supplied assets.

This record does not extend the permission beyond what the conversation
supports:

- future versions must be checked again before they are incorporated;
- third-party content that Tekkit does not have authority to license remains
  excluded;
- the referenced Bebas Kai font is a separate dependency and must not be
  bundled unless its own license permits redistribution; and
- the exact upstream pack and asset revisions must be pinned and audited before
  a translated package is released.

Unless Tekkit later requests another format, the project will credit
**Tekkit's Workshop**, link to the
[original pack](https://www.tekkitsworkshop.net/markers/all-in-one-marker-pack),
and identify the translated or modified text.
